    Tableau Project

    Portland, OR AirBnB (2024-2025) Project - CLICK ON THE IMAGE ABOVE

    Having a close connection to Portland, I was particularly interested in exploring how Airbnb listings are distributed across the city and how they contribute to the local community. This project allowed me to combine my passion for data analysis with a real-world application, providing valuable insights into a sector that is both economically significant and rapidly evolving. This project aims to create a comprehensive dashboard that visualizes Airbnb data for Portland for the years 2024-2025. The dashboard includes various charts to analyze room type listings, revenue trends, and pricing across neighborhoods.

    Business Questions

    1. What is the distribution of different room types in Portland?
    2. Which room types are most popular in different neighborhoods?
    3. What are the monthly revenue trends for Airbnb in Portland for 2024-2025?
    4. What are the average prices of Airbnb listings in different neighborhoods?

    Dashboard Process

    Step 1: Data Preparation

    Obtaining the Airbnb data for Portland, Oregon, for the years 2024-2025 directly from thE AirBnB website. I ensured the dataset included information on room types, prices, revenue, neighborhoods, and dates. Using tools such as Excel and MySQL to clean the data that removed duplicates, missing values, and standardizing date formats.

    Step 2: Import Data into Tableau

    Afterwards, I connected the cleaned dataset into a CSV/Excel file to Tableau servers by navigating to the “Data” menu and selectng “New Data Source.”

    Step 3: Create the Dashboard

    Next, I would create the indivdiual charts, based on the buisness questions asked. When all the charts have been created after manipultaing the data. Each one was put into the dashboard. You will find the types of charts below:

  • Distinct Count of Room Type Listings
  • Revenue by Month
  • Room Type Price by Neighborhood
  • Price by Neighborhood

    • Step 4: Enhance the Dashboard
    Below are ways I enchaced the dashboard to make it more visually appealing and to show key insght metrics:
  • Interactivity
  • Aesthetics
  • Validation

    • Step 5: Provide Key Insights
    1. Dominance of Entire Home/Apt Listings: The dominance of entire home/apartment listings indicates a strong preference for privacy and space. Marketing efforts could emphasize the benefits of having an entire place to oneself, especially for families or larger groups.

    2. Seasonal Revenue Trends: The trendline added to the revenue chart is helpful. The peak months likely correspond to the summer season, which is typically a high travel period. Understanding these trends can help in planning marketing campaigns and adjusting pricing strategies.

    3. Neighborhood-Specific Strategies: High-priced neighborhoods might benefit from marketing that emphasizes luxury and exclusive experiences. For lower-priced neighborhoods, marketing could highlight value and affordability, perhaps appealing to longer stays or budget travelers.

    4. Price Variation Across Neighborhoods: Significant price variations across neighborhoods suggest a diverse market. High-priced neighborhoods like King and Richmond may be more desirable due to their location or amenities. Lower-priced neighborhoods might attract more budget-conscious travelers or offer different types of accommodations.
    Conclusion
  • Key Takeaways
    1. Strategic Pricing and Marketing: Hosts can use the insights from this analysis to adjust their pricing and marketing strategies according to seasonal trends and neighborhood demand to optimize their revenue.
    2. Investment Opportunities: Potential investors can identify high-performing neighborhoods and room types to make informed decisions about where to invest in new properties.
    3. Enhanced Guest Experience: Understanding the preferences of travelers (e.g., preference for entire home/apartment listings) can help hosts tailor their offerings to better meet guest expectations, potentially leading to higher satisfaction and repeat bookings.
  • Future Considerations:
    1. Impact of Regulatory Changes: Future analyses should consider potential regulatory changes affecting short-term rentals in Portland, as these could significantly impact market dynamics.
    2. Longitudinal Data Analysis: Continuing to monitor and analyze data over time will provide deeper insights into long-term trends and emerging patterns in the Airbnb market.
    3. Competitive Landscape: Examining the competitive landscape, including the impact of other short-term rental platforms and hotels, could offer a more holistic view of the accommodation market in Portland.

    Excel Project

    Tesla Stock Dashboard (2015-2023) - CLICK ON THE IMAGE ABOVE

    I chose to analyze Tesla's stock data for this project due to my deep interest in both financial markets and the innovative impact Tesla has on the automotive and energy industries the last decade. Having closely followed Tesla's journey from a niche electric vehicle manufacturer to a dominant force in the market, I was inspired to delve deeper into the financial aspects that reflect its growth and investor sentiment. This project allowed me to merge my passion for data analysis with my curiosity about market dynamics, ultimately creating a comprehensive dashboard that offers valuable insights into Tesla's stock performance from 2015-2023.

    Skills Used

  • Microsoft Excel: For data manipulation and analysis.
  • Data Collection and Cleaning: Sourcing historical stock data, ensuring its accuracy, and preparing it for analysis.
  • Data Analysis: Identifying patterns, trends, and anomalies within the dataset.
  • Data Visualization: To represent data in a clear and insightful manner.
  • Business Analysis: To derive actionable insights and recommendations based on data trends.

    • Business Questions

    1. What strategies can Tesla replicate to sustain or enhance stock price growth?
    2. What opportunities exist for growth during months with lower average stock prices?
    3. Is there a seasonal pattern in Tesla’s stock price variations?
    4. What caused the substantial increase in Tesla’s stock price between 2019 and 2021?

    Data Methodology

    Step 1: Data Collection

    I gathered Tesla stock data from GitHub, covering the period from 2015 to 2023. Having ensured the dataset includes information on stock volume, high, low, opening, and closing prices.

    Step 2: Data Cleaning:

    After reviewing the dataset for any missing or inconsistent data points. As well as cleaning up the data by removing duplicates and handling any missing values appropriately. I was ready to manipulate the data.

    Step 3: Data Preparation:

    By organizing the data by year and month to facilitate analysis of annual and monthly trends. I was able to create additional columns for average stock prices, including high, low, opening, and closing prices.

    Step 4: Data Visualization:

    Using Microsoft Excel and pivot tables, I utilzied bar charts, line graphs, and other visualizations to represent the data clearly. Highlighting key trends, such as stock volume spikes and price peaks.

    Step 5: Data Dashboard:

    After analyzing and creating the visualizations. Significant business insights were identified and I was able to provide actionable recommendations. Through recommendations, the focus was on areas such as strategic product launches, marketing campaigns, and investment opportunities based on observed trends.

    Insights

    The analysis of Tesla's stock data from 2015 to 2023 provides a comprehensive view of the company's stock performance over the years, revealing key trends and insights that are valuable for strategic decision-making.

  • Strategic Timing: The analysis underscores the importance of timing in Tesla's strategic initiatives, suggesting that product launches and market campaigns should be aligned with periods of increased investor activity and favorable stock trends.
  • Innovative Product Launches: Continuous innovation and introduction of groundbreaking products have been key drivers of Tesla's stock performance. Maintaining this momentum will be crucial for sustaining investor interest and market leadership.
  • Market Expansion: Expanding into new markets and forming strategic partnerships have positively impacted Tesla's stock prices. Future growth strategies should continue to focus on these areas to capitalize on emerging opportunities.

    • Conclusion

    In conclusion, the Tesla Stock Data Analysis provides valuable insights into the factors influencing Tesla's stock performance. By leveraging these insights, Tesla can refine its strategic initiatives, optimize market timing, and sustain its competitive edge in the dynamic EV industry. This project not only showcases my ability to analyze complex data sets but also demonstrates my proficiency in deriving actionable business insights from data, making it a valuable addition to my portfolio.

    1. Stock Volume Trends: The data indicates a significant increase in stock trading volume during 2020 and 2021, suggesting heightened investor interest during these years. This surge can be attributed to Tesla's innovative product launches, expansion into new markets, and overall growth in the EV sector. The trend highlights the importance of strategic announcements and market positioning in driving investor activity.
    2. Yearly Stock Price Fluctuations: A substantial rise in Tesla's stock prices from 2019 to 2021, peaking in 2021, reflects the company's successful strategies and market influence. Key factors contributing to this peak likely include product launches, strategic partnerships, and market expansion efforts. This trend emphasizes the positive impact of innovative and well-timed business strategies on stock performance.
    3. Seasonal Trends: Analysis of seasonal trends shows a noticeable increase in stock prices during the second half of the year. This suggests that Tesla's major product releases and marketing campaigns during this period effectively drive up stock prices. Understanding these seasonal patterns can help Tesla optimize the timing of its major initiatives to maximize market impact.
    4. Monthly Analysis: Monthly data analysis reveals consistency in stock prices with minor fluctuations, indicating a stable market presence for Tesla. However, identifying months with lower average stock prices can provide opportunities for strategic announcements or promotional activities to stimulate growth and investor interest.
